ICT Task 1

In I.T we are studying data visualisation and the problem solving methodology. We were required to collect primary data concerning a topic we are interested in where numerical values could be collected. For this topic I chose to collect my data on broadband. I gathered information about the ISP they chose to sign up with, the speed they get and the type of service they have. Because I had to collect primary data I decided to create an online survey using google forms. My survey consisted of very restricted answers after finding that the internet has a lot of trolls now days. Before changing the answers to only tick and numeric answers I had over 20 results within a few hours. These weren't the type of results I was looking for. I think that overall after I began to collect more results that my survey was a success, I ended up with 53 results and was able to use them in an info graphic to visualise my findings as you can see above. This paricular project was being worked on over the span of just over a month. My progress was recorded onto this gnatt chart. My data can be found here
